bounc

Meet Bounc, a design project that honors craftsmanship
and natural beauty.This boutique brand creates ceramic
and stone pieces inspired by the textures, imperfections,
and stories of nature.

The brand’s identity is rooted in the founder’s
personal history. Blue, the signature color of Bounc,
was the favorite color of the founder’s father — the
person who first instilled in him a passion for creating
objects using materials from the earth.

The result is a visual world that feels grounded,
emotional, and innovative — a tribute to tradition,
reimagined through design.

Lola Studio nailed the vision of Bounc — their designs truly brought my father’s ceramic shop to life.
James Dowler: Bounc`s founder
